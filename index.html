My First HCI Project: InterConnect UCSD App

THE PROBLEM:

International Students often feel at social and academic disconnect. While there are various resources for this community, international students need more efficient access to these opportunities in order to connect with other students, utilize academic resources, and familiarize themselves with the campus because of the language and cultural barriers.


Check out our github!

OUR SOLUTION:

My HCI group and I collectively designed a functional app to empower international students with better access to these opportunities. I participated mostly in the front end design, navigation, and user testing. My initial ideas for the app were the following:

  • join and create meetups with other students
  • connect with a tutor
  • browse through campus clubs
  • direct way to connect with students in the same class
